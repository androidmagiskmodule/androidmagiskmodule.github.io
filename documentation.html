Introduction Welcome to the Android Magisk Module Hub, your go-to platform for discovering, sharing, and enhancing Magisk modules. This documentation provides a comprehensive guide to navigating and utilizing the features of our website. ### 2. Getting Started #### 2.1 Account Creation To access the full range of features, create an account. Simply click "Sign Up," fill in your details, and verify your email. #### 2.2 Logging In Already have an account? Click "Log In" and enter your credentials to access your personalized dashboard. ### 3. Browsing Modules #### 3.1 Explore Discover a plethora of Magisk modules tailored to your needs. Use filters to narrow down options or search by keywords. Click on a module to view details, ratings, and comments. #### 3.2 Download Found a module you like? Hit the "Download" button to add it to your collection. Easily manage and update your modules through your account dashboard. ### 4. Uploading Modules #### 4.1 Create a Module Contribute to the community by uploading your Magisk module. Fill in details like name, description, compatibility, and upload the module file. #### 4.2 Versioning Keep your module updated by adding new versions. Users can easily track and download the latest releases. ### 5. Community Features #### 5.1 Ratings and Reviews Share your thoughts on modules by rating and leaving reviews. Help fellow users make informed decisions. #### 5.2 Discussion Engage with the community by participating in module discussions. Ask questions, provide feedback, and connect with developers. ### 6. Troubleshooting #### 6.1 Common Issues Encountering problems? Visit our troubleshooting section for solutions to common issues. If the problem persists, reach out to our support team. ### 7.
